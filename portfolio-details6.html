Project Details

Foosball Table

For this project we were only told to pick a toy and model it in Inventor, no istructions, no base, everything from scratch. I decided to make a foosball table because i thought it would be easier since we have a few around the school. While it is a pretty simple model, there were some problems along the way that I needed to figure out like how to get the foosmen to stay in one place on the sticks and how to make the handles look decent. Overall it came out pretty nice and I am glad I chose it.
